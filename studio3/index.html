Game Rules

  • This game is designed for two players exclusively.
  • Upon entering the game, each player must select one of three distinct characters.
  • Every character comes with a unique set of skills and attack perks that can be viewed in the character selection screen.
  • A player's turn persists until they either miss a skill or a healing spell proves unsuccessful.
  • Victory is achieved when a player's opponent reaches zero health.

Butch

Butch the goose, armed with his trusty butcher's knife, a family heirloom passed down through generations, can conquer any obstacle. Successful attacks deal an extra 5-10 damage.

Pidgy

Pidgey the pidgeon grew up on the streets, learning to survive on scraps since she was just a small hatchling. Endowed with quick wit and nimble hands, she wields two butter knives adeptly for both defense and offense. Her attacks and healing maneuvers boast a 20% increase in success.

Goat

Goat the bird(?) is an enigmatic character; not much is known about him except for his distinctive goat eyes, making him truly one of a kind. Armed with a baseball bat and operating on just one brain cell, he possesses a unique blend of mystery and simplicity. Goat has a 5% chance that his attacks deliver a deadly, game ending, blow.
